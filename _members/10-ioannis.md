## Interests

His research interests are in computer and communication networks, with emphasis on cellular and wireless networking technologies for 5G/6G systems and Open RAN architectures, the application of deep learning for network optimization, and the design of intelligent intrusion detection systems to enhance the security and resilience of wireless infrastructures.
